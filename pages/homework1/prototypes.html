Tableau Prototypes

Bar Chart

Discussion

Sum of Passenger Count for each GEO Region. Color shows details about Price Category Code. Passenger count is encoded by the length of the bar, so longer bar represent higher passenger count.

In terms of the passenger count, US definitely has a dominant amount, which is about 5 times as big as the second region(Asia). Also, from all regions, we can see that the price that is not low fare has a larger proportion.

Area Chart

Discussion

Sum of Passenger Count for each Activity Period Date Year. Color shows sum of Passenger Count. The marks are labeled by sum of Passenger Count. The view is filtered on Activity Period Date Year, which excludes 2005 and 2019. Passenger count is encoded by the size of the area, so larger area represent higher passenger count.

I used a calculated field to convert the "Activity Period" into a Date value and displayed them by year to make it look more clear. The calculation looks like:

DATEPARSE("YYYYMM", STR([Activity Period]))

Obviously, the passenger count increased every year as time goes by. In the year 2018, the passenger count has increased by about 62% compared to the year 2006.

Pie Chart

Discussion

Boarding Area and sum of Passenger Count. Color shows details about Boarding Area. Size shows sum of Passenger Count. The larger size of area represents higher passenger count. The marks are labeled by Boarding Area and sum of Passenger Count.

Boarding area F has the largest percentage compared to the rest of boarding areas, and it is about twice as large as the second area. The rest of boarding areas share a similar porprotion of passengers. While other boarding area only has a tiny percentage, and only 200 passengers got on board from there.
